<p class="p1"><span class="s1"><b>PROPOSAL</b>:</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">An <b>opensource</b> application that would give institutions a real-time platform to monitor the symptoms of the Coronavirus.</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1"><br>
Talking with doctors in Italy, emerged that it would be really beneficial to have the symptomatic map as soon as possible<span class="Apple-converted-space"> </span></span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">(not so important the spread map: it's too late) to block new infections and intervene with the poorly available resources.</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1"><br>
The application users will input only the symptoms they have (temperature, cough, etc.).<span class="Apple-converted-space"> </span></span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">According to such symptoms the authority will be able to decide if they need or not to be tested or when.</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1"><br>
The main idea of the app is to have a symptomatic map so that authorities will be able to intervene at the right place and<span class="Apple-converted-space"> </span></span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">as soon as possible before the infection spreads and avoid the <b>COLLAPSE OF HEALTH STRUCTURES</b>.</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1"><br>
Because of privacy limitations, probably the mandatory element would be finding government sponsorship</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">Government institutions &amp; Institutes of Health would be the only ones predisposed to manage / analyze data</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1"><br>
</span></p>

<p class="p5"><span class="s1"><b>PROPOSTA:</b></span></p>
<p class="p5"><span class="s1">Un'applicazione <b>open source</b> che fornirebbe alle istituzioni una piattaforma in tempo reale per </span><span class="s4"><b>monitorare la diffusione dei sintomi del Coronavirus</b></span><span class="s1"><b>.</b></span></p>
